About Me

I am a Ph.D. candidate in Computer Science at the University of Massachusetts Lowell (UML), under the supervision of Dr. Yimin Chen. Before joining UML, I worked as a full-time Research Assistant at Southern University of Science and Technology (SUSTech), advised by Dr. Yinqian Zhang.

I am delighted to share that I am preparing to graduate soon and am actively seeking research or academic positions. I look forward to connecting with fellow researchers, academics, and potential collaborators or employers.
