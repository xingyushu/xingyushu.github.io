My name is Xingyu Lyu. I obtained my bachelor's degree of EE from Shanghai University in 2018.

I got a master's degree in the Cyberspace Institute of Advanced Technology, Guangzhou University in 2021

From 2021.7~2022.7, I research on security/privacy problems in blockchain in Southern University of Science and Technology,advised by Prof. Yinqian Zhang.

Now I am the first-year PhD. student in Umass Lowell under guidance of (Ian Chen).

My academic research interests include:

  • Security and privacy of federated learning (FL). My interests in FL are that it is distributed and vulnerable to insider attacks. At the same time, FL is subject to performance and communication constrains. These make it challenging and important to work around both security and system design goals.
  • Mainly research on smart contracts vulnerabilities detection such as Ethereum and DeFi-related security issues, and deeply analyze the MEV mechanism
  • Coining privacy-aware NLP models for medical EHR.

My starting views for research include:

  • Continuous scientific research applied to practice
  • Not limited to the knowledge learned to explore science
  • Do scientific research with humane care
  • Embrace teamwork and respect the work of predecessors
