My academic research interests include:

  • Security and privacy of Federated Learning (FL): My interests in FL are that it is distributed and vulnerable to insider attacks. At the same time, FL is subject to performance and communication constrains. These make it challenging and important to work around both security and system design goals.
  • Combination of LLM/Pre-train model and FL: S&P issues,Edge device performance,etc.
  • AI healthcare:e.g. Coining privacy-aware NLP models for medical EHR.
  • Blockchain security(Collaboration):Mainly research on smart contracts vulnerabilities detection such as Ethereum and DeFi-related security issues, and deeply analyze the MEV mechanism
